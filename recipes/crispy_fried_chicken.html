Crispy Fried Chicken

Description

Fried chicken is a staple food all over the world and for good reason. The simple delicious dish takes at most an hour to make and impresses your frind group.

The dish is incredibly simple too and capable of great variety. You may choose to change the seasonings and batter for a whole of unique flavors depending on what you are in the mood for.

Ingredient

  • Chicken
  • Buttermilk
  • Flour
  • Seasonings

Steps

  1. Make the Breading
  2. Dredge the Chicken
  3. Fry the Chicken
